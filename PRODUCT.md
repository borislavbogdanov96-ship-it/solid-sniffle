# Product

## Register

product

## Users

A couple (Bobby & Nikol) planning their wedding on 11 June 2027 in Niagara Falls. They use this daily to track 50+ tasks, manage dependencies, and coordinate progress - collaboratively via Firebase sync. Mobile-first use (checking tasks on the go) with occasional desktop sessions for the Gantt chart overview.

## Product Purpose

A personal, real-time wedding planning tool that turns an overwhelming task list into a clear, dependency-aware Gantt timeline. Success means the couple wakes up knowing exactly what to do next and nothing falls through the cracks before the big day.

## Brand Personality

Intimate, organised, intentional. "A quiet notebook you actually keep up."

## Anti-references

- Generic wedding-planner beige/blush (Canva templates, The Knot)
- SaaS dashboard blue (startup indigo gradients everywhere)
- Over-decorated fussy (floral SVGs, ornamental dividers)

## Design Principles

1. **Calm intelligence** - the interface knows a lot but whispers it; hierarchy emerges from restraint, not decoration.
2. **Romantic overview, sharp tasks** - the countdown and summary feel like a love letter; the task list and Gantt feel like a professional planner.
3. **State speaks clearly** - every task status (done, active, blocked, overdue) is unambiguous at a glance, using colour and form that persist across light and dark mode.
4. **Motion earns its place** - animations convey state change and content arrival; nothing moves for decoration.
5. **Personal at every scale** - names, dates, and the couple's story ("Flowing Together", Niagara) should feel woven into the UI, not appended.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum; AAA target for body text. Supports `prefers-reduced-motion`. Touch targets 44×44 px minimum. Dark mode system-preference aware with manual toggle.
